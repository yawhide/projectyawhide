About ProjectYawHide

YawHide is a multi platform application.

The goal of ProjectYawHide is to ultimately let its users save money by being able to sort and easily find the best deals in their local grocery flyers.

ProjectYawHide only supports Sobeys for now, but in the very near future, it will support a lot more grocery stores. Canada only as well!
